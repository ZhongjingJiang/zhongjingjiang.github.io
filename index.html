Hello! My name is Zhongjing Jiang (姜中景). I am a postdoctoral scholar at Brookhaven National Laborotary, working with Chongai Kuang. I received my PhD degree from Peking University on June 2022, advised by Jing Li and Tzung-May Fu. I received bachelor degrees in Mathematics and applied mathematics from Beijing Normal University. My research interests lie in chemistry-climate interaction. I am also familiar with 3D atmospheric modeling, including atmospheric chemical transport models such as GEOS-Chem, WRF-Chem, and the coupled earth system model CESM.
